About

The Band

Emerging from the depths of modern metal, I See Red delivers a sonic assault that blends the raw aggression of death metal and contemporary production. Formed with a vision to push boundaries while honoring the genre's roots, the band crafts crushing riffs, thunderous rhythms, and haunting melodies that resonate with both old-school metalheads and new generations.

Drawing inspiration from acts like At the Gates, In Flames, and Gojira, I See Red has carved out a unique sonic identity that's equally comfortable in underground clubs and festival stages across Europe.
